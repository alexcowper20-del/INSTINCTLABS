Common questions

Do I own my website?
Yes — 100%. Once the project is complete, everything is yours. No lock-in, no ongoing dependency on us unless you want it.
What do you need from me to get started?
Just your logo, brand colours, and any text or photos you want on the site. We'll guide you through the rest and can even help with copywriting if needed.
Can you refresh an existing site?
Absolutely. Refreshes and redesigns are quoted based on scope — get in touch and we'll give you an honest estimate.
What's the payment structure?
50% upfront to start the project, 50% on delivery. Simple and fair for both sides.
How long does a website take to build?
Starter sites are typically delivered within 7 days. Business sites take 10–14 days. Premium builds are scoped individually but we always give a clear timeline upfront.
